About the Mod

Welcome to the Official Website for Octocon the 8 player mod for Defcon. The mod is months in the making and wouldn't of been possible without some talented people who are passionate about the game. The mod is still in its infancy and I do suggest you email me, or contact one of us on the discord if any bugs or issues arise.
